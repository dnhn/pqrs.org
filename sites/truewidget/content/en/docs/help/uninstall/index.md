---
title: 'How to uninstall TrueWidget'
weight: 200
simple_list: true
---

Remove TrueWidget from your system as follows.

1.  [Quit TrueWidget](/docs/help/quit/) before uninstallaion.
2.  Open `Applications` folder in Finder, then move TrueWidget to trash.
